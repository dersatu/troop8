About the Troop

Troop 8 meets weekly at Ogden Memorial Presbyterian Church in Chatham. We focus on outdoor skills, merit badges, service projects, and community involvement. Scouts of all ages are welcome!

Get Involved

If you’re interested in joining or learning more, please reach out via email or visit us at a meeting night.
